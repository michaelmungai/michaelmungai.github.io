ABOUT

~ What's up! ~



Welcome to my website. My name is Michael Mungai. I am a 21 year old grapich artist and designer from New York City. I study Communication Design at Parsons School of Design.

I was born in Nairobi, Kenya, and moved to New York in 2001 with my family. From 2001 until 2013 I attended the United Nations International School in midtown Manhattan, where I graduated with an International Baccalaureat Diploma, along with my high school diploma.

My passion is in the visual arts. As a graphic designer, I believe that visual imagery is the most powerful form of communication we encounter in our society. Whether we like it or not, every image we see impacts us in some way, and ultimately has a role in the decisions we make. This is why I want to make sure that visual communication has a positive impact on all our lives.

This is my personal website, created using HTML and CSS. It is a continuous work in progress and will be transforming.
